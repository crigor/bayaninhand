Roles of Volunteer:
1. Segregate collected clothes a few days before the event.
2. Pack collected canned food, noodles, and other goods into plastic bags that will be given away.
3. Give out the packs to victims of the flood.

1. Please be in the venue (Quezon City High School) by 7:00am on June 1, 2011.
2. Please bring extra shirt and water.
3. For any last-minute questions or clarifications, please contact 0917-8785596. Look for June Guzman.
